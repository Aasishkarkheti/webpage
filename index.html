THANGKA

THANGKA

Thangka is a sacred form of Tibetan Buddhist painting on cotton or silk fabric, depicting deities, mandalas, and religious scenes. These intricately detailed artworks serve as visual aids for meditation, spiritual practice, and instruction, while also holding cultural and artistic value beyond their religious significance.
